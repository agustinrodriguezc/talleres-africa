04
End-to-End Process Flows

From vehicle reception
to cash in the bank

Proposed process redesign — all steps are recommendations, not a description of current practice at Talleres África.

Full repair cycle — from vehicle arrival to invoice collection. Decision diamonds are mandatory control points in the proposed AIS.

Record-to-Report (R2R)
In the proposed AIS, monthly closing becomes a five-step automatable procedure: (1) reconcile closed WOs with issued invoices; (2) reconcile warehouse consumption with material receipts; (3) allocate indirect costs to cost centres; (4) generate draft P&L by service area; (5) export accounting file to the external advisory firm. This process — which currently takes weeks — should complete in under two hours. Monthly P&L by area (CC01 Mechanics · CC02 Bodywork & Paint · CC03 Tyres · CC04 Multimedia · CC05 Administration) is the primary output.
Acquire-to-Retire (ATR)
Relevant for the workshop's fixed assets: diagnostic equipment (OBD scanners, ECU programming tools), vehicle lifts, IT infrastructure, and paint booth. The proposed AIS records acquisition, useful life, depreciation schedule (straight-line), warranty periods, and planned disposal. This enables precise cost allocation by service area — critical for Bodywork & Paint, where equipment depreciation is the largest indirect cost. Recommended as a Phase 2 expansion after the core AIS is operational.
